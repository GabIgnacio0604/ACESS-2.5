Privacy Policy

Sta. Cruz High School is committed to protecting your personal information. The data you provide through this portal will only be used for necessary verification to confirm that the person signing up is a real student of Sta. Cruz High School.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, password, and School ID or LRN (Learner Reference Number). This information is strictly used to identify you as a legitimate student and provide secure access to the student portal.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify that you are a legitimate student of Sta. Cruz High School.
  • To provide access to online resources, announcements, and activities.
  • To communicate important updates related to school activities.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Only authorized school administrators have access to your data for the purpose of verifying and managing accounts.

Your Consent

By signing up and using this portal, you agree to allow Sta. Cruz High School to use your provided information solely for account verification and related student services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the Sta. Cruz High School administration office.
